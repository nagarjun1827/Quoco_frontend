A comprehensive guide to mastering the Quoco Hub estimation ecosystem.

Overview

Quoco Hub is an advanced engineering portal designed for construction firms to automate tender analysis and Bill of Quantities (BoQ) estimation. This documentation covers the core modules and workflows required to optimize your pre-construction timeline.

Module 1

Tender Analyser

Utilize conversational AI to dissect complex tender documents and identify critical specifications instantly.

PDF Ingestion: Drag and drop tender PDFs into the analysis bar to initialize the ingestion pipeline.
Automated Summarization: Upon upload, the AI generates a structured summary highlighting Scope, Materials, and Deadlines.
Contextual Chat: Ask specific questions like "What are the liquidated damages?" or "List the required material grades" to receive answers directly from the document source.
Analysis Tip: Use the "Knowledge Base" toggle in the Tender Analyser to view all documents currently in the session's memory.
Module 2

BoQ Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base serves as your firm's institutional memory, archiving past Bills of Quantities for historical pricing reference.

Archive Management: Store historical BoQs categorized by individual project names.
Project Tagging: When uploading archives, provide a project identifier to enable efficient filtering and lookup.
Data Preservation: Metadata for every file (size, upload date, file type) is persisted to ensure full auditability of your pricing history.
Module 3 (PRO)

BoQ Estimator

Our flagship AI tool for generating predictive cost estimates for new construction projects based on material parameters and historical curves.

Estimation Queue: Monitor active estimation requests through a centralized tracking table.
Status Lifecycle: Track tasks through four stages: Queued, In Progress, Success, and Failed.
Deeper Insights: AI mapping correlates material quantities with global market indices to provide high-fidelity cost breakdowns.
Pro Tip: Ensure your BoQ files are in .xlsx format for the highest precision during AI parameter mapping.

Settings & Preferences

Customize your experience and manage organizational visibility.

Theme Controls: Switch between High-Contrast Dark and Vibrant Light modes in the Settings panel.
Logout: Securely end your session through the standardized red Action Button in the global navigation bar.
